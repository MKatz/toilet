Traditional vs Low-Flush Toilets


Find Out More

Installing a low-flush toliet (high efficiency) would save 19% of your indoor water use.

Often, we focus on curtailment solutions, such as shorter showers, or turning off the water while brushing out teeth. The effieciency solution, installing a low-flush toliet, is ignored due to our bias toward the cost of a one-time expense. However, over time the wasted water will end up costing more than buying a new low-flush toliet.
